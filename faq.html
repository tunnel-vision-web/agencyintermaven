Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our free consultations, AI-assisted delivery pipeline, and credit ecosystem.

What happens during the free initial consultation?
Every engagement starts with a free, 30-minute consultation with a real tech lead or account manager. We review your vision, technical requirements, and map your project timeline before scoping.
How does the AI-assisted pipeline differ from traditional agencies?
AI handles repetitive tasks like component scaffolding, boilerplate generation, and asset processing. Strategic direction, UI design polish, and QA are strictly directed by seasoned human strategists.
What is the difference between Done-For-You and Self-Serve?
Done-For-You is a full agency engagement where our studio handles end-to-end strategy, design, and code. Self-Serve (via intermaven.io) allows founders to run on our credit system directly.
How are leads and bookings managed in your system?
All appointment bookings and newsletter signups double-opt-in directly into our Smart CRM backend admin, notifying assigned tech leads instantly.
